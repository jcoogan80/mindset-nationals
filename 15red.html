Parking Near the Convention Center

Downtown garages fill fast on tournament weekends — arrive early or pre-book on SpotHero / ParkWhiz. Many connect to the Convention Center via the enclosed skywalk.

Pan Am Plaza Garage
201 S Capitol Ave · skywalk-connected · closest to the ICC halls. ~$15–25/day.
Circle Centre Mall Garages
Maryland St & Washington St entrances · skywalk to the ICC · plenty of spaces.
Capitol Commons Garage
10 N Capitol Ave · underground · ~2 blocks north, walkable.
White River State Park Garage
801 W Washington St · larger lot, usually cheaper · ~10 min walk west.
Rideshare & Street
Uber/Lyft drop-off on Georgia St; pickup at the south entrance. Metered street parking via the ParkIndy app (free Sundays).
